Succession Advice

Business Broker AG has already supported more than 300 successions. We know the specific demands and can offer competent advice in all areas.

Sooner or later each entrepreneur must think about its successor. Business Broker AG recommends carrying out a „Check-Up“ at the latest 5 years before the planned succession. We screen your companies in all aspects and offer specific recommendations.

The following areas are analysed:

  • Company structure / ownership structure
  • Fiscal situation / tax consequences
  • Balance structure (Ready to sell)
  • Analysis of the processes / markets / customers
  • Company evaluation
  • Salebility / possibilties

The successor check up creates the perfect basis for you to set the correct points at the right time and guarantees the successful company successor.
