Strategic Acquisitions – Merger & Acquisition (M&A) – SME

Mergers & Acquisitions (M & A) gives companies the opportunity to grow by organic growth and through acquisitions. The M & A (merger and acquisition) sector offers many opportunities for small and medium-sized companies. However, smaller companies are still only using this opportunity to a limited degree.

The benefits of growth through acquisition lie in the fact that growth targets are implemented geographically and/or sector-oriented quicker and more efficient and can thus support the defined growth strategy. Reasons for such a strategy could be:

  • Take over competitors
  • Open new distribution channels
  • Expand your portfolio
  • Gain new know-how
  • Use of economies of scale
  • Gain market power
  • Cost-effective production opportunities
  • Generate new procurement opportunities, etc.
