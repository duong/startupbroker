With the free search subscription from Business Broker AG you can be automatically notified by e-mail about new listings that match your search criteria.

You can specify a region and/or industry and/or price range as search criteria. If you do not limit these three categories further, your Search Subscription is valid for all newly listed properties. You have the option of defining multiple search subscriptions.
