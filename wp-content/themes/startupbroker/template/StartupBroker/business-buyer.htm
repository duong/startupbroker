The club for business people

Business Broker AG is the largest and most successful consulting and placement company in the small and medium sized companies sector. Currently Business Broker AG is looking for a buyer for more than 270 successful companies. Currently we are taking over new and attractive sales mandates.

Business Buyer aligns itself with serious and solvent persons who want a target orientated approach to company acquisition. The Business Buyer Club creates decisive advantages for its members with respect to non members.

Only members receive access to all Business Broker AG offers. They are informed about new sales mandates at least 4 weeks before the official marketing. The weekly Business Buyer Report delivers exclusive data about the current projects (location, sectors, number of employees, turnover).

  • Access to all sales offers of Business Broker AG
  • Information at least 4 weeks before the official marketing
  • Weekly Business Buyer Report

  • Accelerated purchasing process
